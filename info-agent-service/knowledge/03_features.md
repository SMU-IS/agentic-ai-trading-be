# Agent M Features

## Core Intelligence
*   **Autonomous Trading:** Agent M continuously monitors market news, runs sentiment analysis, and executes optimized buy and sell orders through your **Alpaca Brokerage Account**—all without you lifting a finger.
*   **News Intelligence:** Agent M scrapes Reddit, Yahoo Finance, Bloomberg, and more—then scores each article for sentiment and credibility.
*   **Credibility & Fact-Checking:** Every signal is cross-referenced against multiple sources. The system assigns a **Source Reliability Score** to ensure that only verified "sauce" drives trade decisions.
*   **Sentiment-Weighted Signals:** Processes real-time data to provide "top sentiment signals" for your specific watchlist, weighting institutional reports higher than social media rumors.

## Risk & Portfolio Management
*   **Granular Risk Controls:** Configure max position sizes, drawdown limits, and sector caps. Agent M will never breach your limits—no matter how strong the signal.
*   **Smart Alerts:** Get notified the instant breaking news directly impacts a stock you hold—and receive trade confirmations the moment Agent M acts on your behalf.
*   **Automated Guardrails:** Users can set specific constraints, such as:
    > "Set my max single-position size to 8% and pause trading if daily loss exceeds 3%."
*   **Conflict Resolution:** The system automatically checks for opposing positions or pending orders before execution, preventing redundant or conflicting trades.

## Interactive Experience (AskAI)
*   **RAG-Driven Chatbot:** Ask anything about your holdings, past trades, or market events. Agent M retrieves verified news and portfolio context using Retrieval-Augmented Generation to give you grounded, specific answers.
*   **Natural Language Trade Search:** Locate trades in your history using plain English. No need for cryptic order IDs.
    *   **Temporal Search:** "Why did you sell GOOGL last week?" (Resolves to the correct date range).
    *   **Ticker Discovery:** "Why u sell google?" (Searches your last 30 days for fuzzy matches).
    *   **Contextual References:** "Tell me more about the first one." (Resolves ordinal selections from history).
*   **Plain Language Explanations:** Your portfolio is explained in simple terms, answering complex questions like:
    > "Why did Agent M sell my TSLA position last week?"
*   **Dynamic Visual Feedback:** High-fidelity visual cards for every action, from clean trade lists to deep-dive technical reasoning chains.
